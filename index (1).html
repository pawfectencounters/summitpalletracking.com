Company Overview

From the Inland Empire to Texas

Summit Industrial Group LLC was founded in 2020 and is headquartered in City of Industry, California. We're a professional warehouse racking services company specializing in installation, teardown & recycling, and new and used rack sales. From day one we've been rooted in the Inland Empire, serving warehousing, logistics, and manufacturing clients across Chino, Ontario, Fontana, City of Industry, and beyond — building our reputation on solid fieldwork and transparent, itemized pricing.

Over the years our footprint has grown from the Inland Empire to cover the entire Greater Los Angeles and Orange County region, and we've now formally expanded into the Houston, Texas market, offering a one-stop solution — from material procurement and design through on-site installation — for cross-state warehouse projects. We remain committed to bilingual communication, engineering-grade installation standards, and complete racking solutions for every client, with the goal of being the long-term partner warehouse owners trust across Southern California and Texas.

Core Services

Six Services, One Full Rack Lifecycle

From early planning through teardown and recycling, we own every step in-house — no need to coordinate multiple vendors.

Installation

From on-site assessment and rack selection to layout planning and final installation — including permit-ready technical drawings when required, designed around your ceiling height, load requirements, and forklift traffic.

Removal & Recycling

Standardized teardown, sorting, and loadout that minimizes disruption to your operations. Removed racking can be bought back and credited against labor, lowering your disposal cost.

New & Used Rack Sales

Uprights, beams, wire decking, and other core materials in stock year-round, new or used. Used inventory is sourced from teardown jobs and auctions, inspected before resale.

Security Camera Installation

Installed alongside racking projects so you can complete equipment upgrades and security improvements on a single project timeline.

Layout Planning

Layout recommendations based on your ceiling height, load requirements, and equipment, balancing storage density with operational efficiency.

Permit Documentation Support

Honest disclosure of material sourcing and specs on every job, working with your Engineer of Record to prepare compliant permit documentation.
